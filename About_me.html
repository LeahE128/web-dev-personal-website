I am a biotechnologist with a Double Honours BSc in Biology and Chemistry from Maynooth University (achieved a 2.1) and an MSc in Biotechnology from UCD (achieved a 1.1). The last two years I worked in a biotechnology company called Nuritas who combine AI and genomics to predict molecules in food that have a high probability of having health benefits. Working in Nuritas exposed me to the area of AI in the health tech industry and I discovered I had an interest in this area. I began an MSc in Computer Science in UCD this year with the aim of pursuing a role in the health tech field after completion.
